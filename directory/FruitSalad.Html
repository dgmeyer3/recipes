Fruit Salad

This appealing fruit salad is a lovely addition to breakfast, lunch or even supper.
Light and refreshing, it's perfect alongside egg bakes, sausages and other hearty
staples you find on breakfast buffets.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (20 ounces) pineapple chunks
  • 2 large firm bananas, cut into 1/4-inch chunks
  • 1 cup green grapes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) mandarin oranges, drained
  • 1 medium red apple, sliced
  • 1 medium green apple, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Drain pineapple, reserving juice. Combine the pineapple, bananas, grapes, oranges
    and apples in a large bowl; set aside.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine sugar and cornstarch. Add the orange juice, lemon
    juice and reserved pineapple juice; stir until smooth. Bring to a boil; reduce
    heat. Cook and stir for 2 minutes. Pour over fruit; mix gently. Cover and
    refrigerate until serving.
